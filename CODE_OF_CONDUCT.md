# Code of Conduct

The aozora-tools project adopts the
[**Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as its code of conduct. Participation in this project — as a contributor,
a reviewer, a maintainer, or a participant in the issue tracker or
discussions — means agreeing to the expectations laid out at the link
above.

The authoritative text is maintained at contributor-covenant.org and is
not reproduced here; please read it at the link above rather than relying
on any partial summary.

## Enforcement

Reports of conduct violations should reach the maintainers privately
through one of:

1. A **GitHub private security advisory** at
   <https://github.com/P4suta/aozora-tools/security/advisories/new>.
   Although security advisories are normally the channel for vulnerability
   reports, they also accept private conduct reports — please mark
   the report title clearly as a conduct concern.
2. Email to the maintainer at `42543015+P4suta@users.noreply.github.com`
   with the subject `[aozora-tools conduct] <short summary>`.

Please **do not open public issues** for personal conduct complaints.
Maintainers will acknowledge reports within seven days and will keep the
reporter's identity confidential to the extent consistent with acting on
the report.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to every aozora-tools project space — the
issue tracker, pull requests, discussions, documentation, the rustdoc
site, and any ancillary channel operated on the project's behalf.
